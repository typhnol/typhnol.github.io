i'm typhnol (he/him), typh shortened, an average internet lurker. generally i'll love any game that's fun and has a good story. i'll listen to anything that sounds good to my ears. you can find media/games/music im into, as well as some fun blinkies and buttons to sites I like or found interesting on this page.

what's this?

just a personal website handcoded by yours truly. it's kinda shit but we ball. back in january 2024 i saw that one of my favorite artists at the time, mothCPU, had a neocities page, and i got curious. previously i used a carrd as a personal website to introduce myself, but seeing that you could code your own website to whatever you want was really an amazing thing, and i was very inspired by these many neocities websites by other people. i put quite some effort into this website and you can see the many iterations of layouts that this site went through lol (found in art > old layouts).

why? 🤨

well, why not? i had some experience with HTML and CSS all the way back in 2020-2021 from a coding class at school, and then some Javascript in another coding class, and with a whole lot of help from W3Schools and Stack Overflow, i managed to make the amalgamation that is this site. also, it's fun. wouldn't you like a little personal corner of the internet that you can customize to your heart's content and where you can share all sorts of things or make funny little interactive widgets?
